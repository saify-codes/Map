Buffalo, New York

A City of Innovation, Art, and Rich History

City Information

Buffalo, located in western New York, is known for its impressive architecture, proximity to Niagara Falls, and revitalized waterfront area. It has a rich industrial past and is a city undergoing significant economic and cultural renewal.

Key Facts about Buffalo

Population

As of the 2020 census, Buffalo has a population of approximately 278,000 people.

Year Incorporated

Buffalo was incorporated as a city in 1832.

Region

Buffalo is located in the Western New York region of the state.

Classification

Buffalo is an urban city known for its industrial history and cultural institutions.

Average Income

The average income in Buffalo is below the state average, with a median household income of about $40,000 compared to the state's average of $70,000.
