Syracuse, New York

A City of Education, Technology, and Cultural Richness

City Information

Syracuse, located in Central New York, is known for its excellent universities, large educational institutions, and its vibrant downtown area. It is a key player in technology, agriculture, and the arts within the region.

Key Facts about Syracuse

Population

As of the 2020 census, Syracuse has a population of approximately 142,000 people.

Year Incorporated

Syracuse was incorporated as a city in 1848.

Region

Syracuse is located in the Central New York region of the state.

Classification

Syracuse is an urban city, serving as a central hub for education and technology in the region.

Average Income

The average income in Syracuse is around $50,000, which is lower than the state average of $70,000.
